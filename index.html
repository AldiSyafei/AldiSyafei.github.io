How to Make Simple Fried Rice


Ingredient :
1. 1 portion of rice
2. 2 cloves of garlic
3. 3 cloves of onion
4. Salt
5. Pepper
6. Fried Oil

Tools :
1. Spatula
2. Teflon
3. Stoves
4. Plate
5. Spoon
6. Knife
7. Chopper Board

Steps :
1. First, Chop the garlic and onion into small piece
2. And then, Stir-fry the red onion and the garlic until you can smell the scent
3. After that, Pour in the rice and stir until evenly distributed
4. Then, add soy sauce, salt and pepper and mix it again
5. Afterward, wait until cooked evenly
6. Finally, serve on a plate
